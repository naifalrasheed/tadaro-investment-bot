Configuration Guide

Component Descriptions
  • Price Momentum (0-100%):

    Measures recent price movement trends based on 5-day, 10-day, and 20-day price movements.

  • 52-Week Range (0-100%):

    Where the current price sits in the 52-week trading range. Higher score when closer to 52-week high.

  • YTD Performance (0-100%):

    Year-to-date percentage return. Higher scores for better performance.

  • News Sentiment (0-100%):

    Analysis of recent news articles sentiment from financial news and social media.

  • ROTC (0-100%):

    Return on Total Capital, a fundamental metric measuring company's efficiency.

Weight Guidelines
  • All weights must sum to exactly 100%
  • Set any component to 0% to ignore it completely
  • Higher weights indicate higher importance
  • The weights determine how sentiment scores are calculated
